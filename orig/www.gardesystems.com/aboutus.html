About Garde Systems

Garde Systems was founded in 2007 by Wayne Guerra and Lincoln
Gup. Wayne Guerra is a practicing emergency medicine physician as
well as Vice President of Operations of Serio Physician Management
LLC. Lincoln Gup has 15 years experience driving revenue in the
high-tech industry through the collaborative leadership of product
development efforts. Both principals received their MBAs from the
University of Denver. They can be contacted at:
Wayne Guerra, MD, MBA
Lincoln Gup, MBA
Our Business Philosophy

We are dedicated to the use of unique, innovative technology to
contribute to advancing quality-of-care in the out-of-hospital and
in-hospital settings. We find that by creating a learning environment
where employees love their work, our customers receive excellent
service in return. It is our intention to encourage and aid our
employees to reach their full potential and improve their standard of
living. And, when the business profits, the wealth is shared with our
employees and the community.
Our Mission

Garde Systems provides safety solutions and services for both
out-of-hospital and in-hospital medical care providers.
