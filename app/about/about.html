Background

Garde Systems was founded in 2007 by Wayne Guerra and Lincoln Gup. Wayne Guerra is an emergency medicine physician and serial entrepreneur with a focus on technologies that improve the patient-physician interaction. Lincoln Gup has 15 years experience driving revenue in the high-tech industry through the collaborative leadership of product development efforts. Both principals received their MBAs from the University of Denver.
